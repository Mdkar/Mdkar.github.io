**iOS app currently in development**

This app lets users "buy stocks" of professional sports players (currently just NBA players) while competing with their friends to make the biggest profit in game. The app consists of a social aspect, letting users sign up, send and accept friend requests, send messages and challenges with notifications (in progress). The trading aspect of the game lets users submit buy and sell orders, while a trading engine on the backend fulfills them.

Front end: React-Native using Expo
Back end: Node.js

Made in collaboration with [Himanshu Biradar](https://www.linkedin.com/in/himanshu-biradar-7a4b601b5/) and others
